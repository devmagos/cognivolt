Who We Are

Reimagining How African Fleets Think

Cognivolt Mobility is a Lagos-based automotive AI company building the intelligence layer that bridges the gap between raw vehicle telemetry and the data-driven decisions that make fleets profitable, safe, and investable.

We deploy a suite of real-time ML models — processing OBD-II data, GPS streams, and driver behaviour signals — to give fleet operators, logistics companies, and mobility investors a live, predictive picture of their entire vehicle ecosystem.

From ride-hailing operators in Lagos to long-haul logistics firms spanning all of West Africa, Cognivolt turns raw vehicle data into competitive advantage and investor-grade performance reporting.
