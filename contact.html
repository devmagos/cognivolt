Common Questions

Before You Write to Us

Quick answers to what investors and fleet operators ask most often.

How long does onboarding take?

Most fleets are fully onboarded within 3–5 business days including hardware installation, data calibration, and dashboard training.

What vehicles does Cognivolt support?

Any vehicle with an OBD-II port (all post-1996 vehicles) plus commercial trucks and buses via our CAN bus adapters.

Is there a minimum fleet size?

We work with fleets from 10 vehicles. Pricing scales with fleet size — contact us for a tailored commercial proposal.

Do you operate outside Lagos?

Yes — Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano, with Ghana and Kenya expansion planned for Q3 2026.

How is our fleet data protected?

All data is on AWS af-south-1 (Lagos) with AES-256 encryption, NDPC compliance, and full data sovereignty within Nigeria.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — qualifying fleets are offered a 30-day pilot at no cost. Message us via the form above with your fleet size.
