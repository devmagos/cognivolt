Engineering Principles

How We Build

Four non-negotiable principles that govern every engineering decision at Cognivolt.

🌍

Africa-First Performance

Every system is benchmarked against real Nigerian road conditions, 3G connectivity, and power interruptions. If it doesn't work reliably in Lagos traffic, it doesn't ship.

🔍

Explainable AI

Every recommendation surfaces its confidence score and contributing factors. Fleet managers and investors see exactly why the system flagged a fault — no black boxes accepted.

🛡️

Data Sovereignty

All fleet data is stored within Nigerian borders on AWS Lagos (af-south-1). Operators retain full ownership with export and deletion rights enforced at the API level — NDPC compliant.

🔄

Continuous Learning

Our models retrain monthly on the latest fleet outcomes. Every fault prediction, route recommendation, and driver event feeds back into improving accuracy — the platform gets smarter with every season.
