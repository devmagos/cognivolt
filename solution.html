Our Solution

Six Pillars of Fleet Intelligence

One unified AI platform covering the full operational lifecycle — from engine fault detection to investor-grade performance reporting. Every module deploys independently or as a fully integrated suite.

Deep Dive

What We Build for You

Each module is independently deployable — built for fleets of 10 to 10,000 vehicles, in any sector.

01

🔮 Predictive Maintenance AI

Our ML models process OBD-II telemetry, vibration, and thermal data to detect fault signatures up to 14 days before failure with 98% accuracy.

  • Monitors 200+ engine and drivetrain parameters per second
  • Sends manager alerts 14 days before predicted failure
  • Integrates with workshop booking and parts ordering
  • Reduces unplanned downtime by up to 60%
02

🛣️ Dynamic Route Optimisation

Traffic, road conditions, weather, and load data feed our routing engine — continuously recalculating the optimal path for each active vehicle.

  • Sub-second route recalculation on traffic events
  • Reduces average journey time by 22–28%
  • Fuel savings of 18–35% on optimised corridors
  • Multi-stop delivery sequencing for logistics
03

👁️ Driver Behaviour Analytics

In-cabin computer vision and telematics score every driver across safety, efficiency, fatigue, and compliance in real time.

  • Detects drowsiness, phone use, and harsh braking
  • Live driver score on fleet command dashboard
  • Auto-generates coaching reports for managers
  • Insurance-compatible data exports for premium reduction
04

📊 Fleet Command Dashboard

Real-time, single-pane-of-glass view of location, health, fuel, and driver score for every vehicle — live on web and mobile.

  • Sub-second GPS refresh for all active vehicles
  • Geofence alerts and route deviation notifications
  • Role-based access for drivers, managers, executives
  • Historical playback for any journey and incident
05

⚡ EV Charge & Range Planning

For electric fleets — charge scheduling that maximises battery longevity, prevents stranded vehicles, and minimises energy costs.

  • Predicts range based on route, load, and weather
  • Off-peak charge scheduling cuts energy costs 28%
  • Battery degradation modelling extends pack life
  • Integration with major EV charging network APIs
06

📡 IoT Sensor Integration

Plug-and-play OBD-II dongles and CAN bus adapters retrofit any vehicle into a fully connected data-streaming asset in under 30 minutes.

  • Universal OBD-II compatibility for any post-1996 vehicle
  • CAN bus adapters for commercial trucks and buses
  • 72-hour local data buffer for offline resilience
  • 4G/LTE and LoRaWAN dual connectivity options
